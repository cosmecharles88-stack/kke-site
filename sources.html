1. AMBI-JACK 4.0 Tested Polyurethane Foam

KKE uses AMBI-JACK 4.0 polyurethane foam for concrete lifting and stabilization.

  • Density: 4.0–4.4 lb/ft³, ASTM D1622
  • Compressive Strength: 70–80 psi, ASTM D1621
  • Closed Cell Content: >85%, ASTM D1940
  • Water Absorption: <0.02%, ASTM D570
  • Strength Development: 90% compressive strength within 30 minutes
  • Referenced Testing: TxDOT 3086, NYSDOT GTP-9, ALDOT-434-09

2. Property Management & Maintenance Benefits

  • • Corrects settled concrete without full demolition
  • • Reduces tenant disruption and downtime
  • • Helps eliminate trip hazards at walkways, entries, and high-traffic areas
  • • Fills hidden voids under slabs and panels
  • • Helps restore slope and drainage
  • • Supports proactive maintenance instead of emergency repair cycles

3. ADA & Trip-Hazard Support

ADA Section 303 addresses changes in level on walking surfaces. This supports the importance of correcting uneven sidewalks, entrances, parking access routes, and tenant-facing walkways.
