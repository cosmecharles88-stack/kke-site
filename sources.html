1. AMBI-JACK 4.0 (Tested & Certified Polyurethane)

KKE uses AMBI-JACK 4.0 polyurethane foam, a slab lifting material engineered for structural support, void filling, and soil stabilization.

  • Density: 4.0–4.4 lb/ft³ (ASTM D1622)
  • Compressive Strength: 70–80 psi (ASTM D1621)
  • Closed Cell Content: >85% (ASTM D1940)
  • Water Absorption: <0.02% (ASTM D570)
  • Strength Development: ~90% within 30 minutes
  • DOT References: TxDOT 3086, NYSDOT GTP-9, ALDOT-434-09
